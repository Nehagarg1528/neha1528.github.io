      ABOUT VADODRA

      Vadodara is famous for Patola silk sarees, Bandhnis and traditional ghagras, Embroidered quilts, Bangles, Khadi, Jewelry. What is Vadodara famous for? This city is known for the famous Lakshmi Vilas Palace which is the residence of state's Maratha Royal Family.adodara has witnessed establishment of medium and large scale industries. With great strides in economic field, the city has giant industrial complexes and public undertaking like Gujarat Refinery, Indian Petrochemicals, Gujarat State Fertilizers, Heavy Water Project, Oil & Natural Gas Commission etc.Dhuliram Penda is famous sweet in Vadodara.
