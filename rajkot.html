      ABOUT RAJKOT

      Rajkot is the fourth largest city in the state of Gujarat, India, after Ahmedabad, Surat, and Vadodara. Rajkot is The Center of the Saurashtra region of Gujarat. Rajkot is the 35th-largest urban agglomeration in India with a population more than 1.2 million as of 2015. Rajkot is the ninth cleanest city of India. It is now an important commercial and industrial centre. The manufacture of cotton and woolen textiles is a major activity; ceramics, diesel engines, and water pump sets are also produced in the city.
