ABOUT SURAT

Surat is also known to be the world's largest diamond manufacturing centre, home to over 5,000 diamond manufacturing units including most advanced, large-scale diamond cutting factories in the world. The Surat SEZ boasts of over 100 listed companies and is fast emerging as a leading jewellery production hub.'I have enough clothes or diamonds' said no woman ever, especially while visiting the city regarded as the hub of these. Nestled in the bustling state of Gujarat, Surat is one of the most dynamic cities in India. Known as the centre of textiles in India, the Surat market is famous for its food, fabric, and diamonds.
